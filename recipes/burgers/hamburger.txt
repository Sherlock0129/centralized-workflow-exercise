# Classic Hamburger Recipe

## Ingredients
- 500g ground beef (80% lean / 20% fat)
- 4 hamburger buns
- 1 large onion, sliced
- 4 slices of cheese (cheddar or American)
- 4 lettuce leaves
- 1 large tomato, sliced
- Ketchup and mustard
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and form patties.
2. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Cook the patties for 3-4 minutes on each side for medium doneness.
5. Add cheese slices on top of patties in the last minute of cooking.
6. Toast the hamburger buns lightly.
7. Assemble burgers: bottom bun, lettuce, tomato, patty with cheese, onion, condiments, top bun.
8. Serve immediately while hot.

## Notes
- For juicier burgers, don't press down on the patties while cooking.
- Add bacon for extra flavor.
